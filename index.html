Developer

Sellers Club

iOS, Android, and Web

Challenge: There isn't a great online community focused solely on the sales profession. We set out to create one. To pull this off I needed to create a platform that would allow our research division the ability to easily add new content to the community, protect certain IP, tie into a corporate identity management solution, and I had to create a platform to deliver this content in just under 3 months.

Solution: I co-designed and built a WordPress site to host the content and manage the content authors. This was the platform the authors and researchers were already familiar with. I then designed and built an Angular mobile app that uses a REST API to sync content between WordPress and the mobile app. Both the web and mobile apps used OAuth2/JWT to authorize and authenticate against our in-house IdP.

Result: (releasing publicly 2018Q1)

Technology: Angular, Ionic, JavaScript, WordPress, PHP, OAuth2, APIs, HTML, CSS

REACH Conference App

iOS and Android

Challenge: We needed an app that would allow us to interact with our customers at our national conference, as well as offer all the traditional benefits: agenda, speaker info, event map, social network promotions, networking with other attendees, etc.

Solution: I rapidly developed a hybrid app for iOS and Android that included these features.

Result: Not needing to purchase a white label app and pay additional costs to add features, we were able to use our app budget for more activities for our attendees to enjoy.

Technology: PhoneGap + Cordova plugins, JavaScript, HTML, CSS

Tailor and Measure

Web

Challenge: Our process for delivering customized materials was completely manual which meant it was costly and required a full-time employee to manage.

Solution: Staying within the company's existing design guidelines, I designed and built a web portal that allowed our customers to build a custom set of training materials to use in their corporate trainings.

Result: Automating the customization tool for our customers freed up a full-time employee to handle something other than the logistics of shipping customization kits. It also saved us the material and shipping costs. Plus, our customers loved the convenience of being able to simply login and download the materials they required.

Technology: PHP, jQuery, HTML, CSS, Amazon AWS

WordPress Network (Multisite)

Web

Challenge: Five business units all needed to quickly update their branding and websites after a series of mergers and acquisitions. We only had two people to create the new websites – myself and a copywriter. We also had to maintain the parent company's website and branding.

Solution: I created a WordPress network and based allites off of a single theme. Updates, maintenance, and user control is all done once instead of six times.

Result: A two-man team managed to pull off all of the work. Although several of the business units have taken over design and maintenance of their websites, my ability to manage users, updates, and handle technology conflicts is still a huge benefit.

Technology: WordPress, JavaScript, PHP, HTML, CSS
