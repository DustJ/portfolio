Goal

Help our customers get the right information, at the right time, with little friction.

Challenges
  1. Managing your insurance is so infrequent that users never become familiar with the process.
  2. Insurance regulations limit user-friendly language.
  3. We were on an outdated tech stack that was never meant to handle the amount of .
  4. The UX and Front-End teams were separate from the Backend and DevOps teams.
Kickoff

Customers were calling our support staff out of frustration.

We had a portal for our customers to manage, review, and pay for their insurance policies—all in one convenient place. However our support call volume was increasing and every new insurance product we created required additional support reps. We couldn't scale like this.

During the kickoff meeting I walked the execs through a series of UX best practices and current UI trends we could use to improve the experience for our users, but regulations ruled out a good chunk of our ideas. DevOps then ruled out any remaining solutions my team had come up with. Like most things software-related in the insurance world, the entire tech stack was old.
